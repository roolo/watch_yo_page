Watch Yo Page

WYP allow you to watch your favorite page for changes. Once the page changes, You'll receive Yo with link to the changed page. You tell WYP to watch a page by sending it link from within Yo app.

Get Yo

Once You have Yo app installed, send link to user WATCHYOPAGE. You can do that by sharing from Your mobile browser via Yo app. If You have link to page in Your mobile clipboard, You can also hold WATCHYOPAGE in the Yo app and it'll do the same.
